A Collective Experience of Chip Shortage for Media Artists

Chip shortage is a global phenomenon that has affected the electronics industry during the pandemic. It is a complex issue that involves multiple factors, include but not limited to supply chain disruptions, geopolitical tensions, and natural disasters.
Media art is also not immune to the effects of the chip shortage, as it is a crucial component in the production of digital art. ESP, Raspberry-Pi, Arduino, Various types of sensors, and all other microcontrollers, anything that are related to electronics commodities, are all affected by the chip shortage. This collective database serves as a platform for artists to share their experiences and challenges in the face of this global phenomenon.
