A Collective Experience of Chip Shortage for Media Artists

Chip shortage is a global phenomenon that has affected the electronics industry during the pandemic. It is a complex issue that involves multiple factors, including supply chain disruptions, geopolitical tensions, and natural disasters.
Media art is also not immune to the effects of the chip shortage. This collective database serves as a platform for artists to share their experiences and challenges in the face of this global phenomenon.
