A Collective Experience of Chip Shortage for Media Artists

The global chip shortage has significantly impacted the electronics industry during the pandemic. This complex issue is attributed to multiple factors, including supply chain disruptions, geopolitical tensions, and natural disasters.
Media art has not been spared from the effects of the chip shortage, as it relies heavily on digital components in its production. Microcontrollers like ESP, Raspberry-Pi, Arduino, and various sensors, which are essential for digital art, have also been affected by the shortage. This collective database aims to provide a platform for artists to share their experiences and challenges in the face of this global phenomenon.
